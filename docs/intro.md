# Welcome to the Physical-AI-Humanoid-Robotics-Future Textbook

This textbook explores the exciting field of Physical Artificial Intelligence (Physical AI) and its applications in humanoid robotics.

## About This Book

This book is designed for AI practitioners and students who want to understand how artificial intelligence operates in physical environments through embodied machines.

## Chapter Overview

- **Chapter 1**: Foundations of Physical AI - Introduces core concepts of Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- **Chapter 2**: ROS 2 as Robotic Nervous System - Learn how to use ROS 2 for robot control
- **Chapter 3**: Digital Twins with Simulation Tools - Explore simulation environments
- **Chapter 4**: Vision-Language-Action Systems - Learn about modern AI for robot actions
- **Chapter 5**: Capstone Project - Putting it all together in a complete system

This textbook follows a progressive learning approach, building on concepts from one chapter to the next.
